[SonicSift](https://www.sonicsift.com) is a music news aggregator that collects the best music journalism from across the web.

Instead of checking a dozen different sites, SonicSift brings it all together in one clean feed. Focused on quality writing about music — reviews, interviews, features, and discovery.

A simple tool for music nerds who want to stay informed without the noise.
